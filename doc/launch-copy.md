## Demo Subtitle Script

### English

1. This is a local-first lead capture and quote follow-up tool.
2. Start in Lead Capture and find company websites worth contacting.
3. Move one lead into follow-up with one click.
4. It becomes a Customer, Quote, and Task in your local pipeline.
5. Use Today Queue to see what to push first.
6. Use Quick Follow-up to keep the quote moving.
7. Review every change in Timeline, and undo the last AI update if needed.
8. Data stays local. External AI APIs are off by default.

### 中文

1. 这是一款本地优先的找客与报价推进工具。
2. 从 Lead Capture 开始，先找到真正值得联系的公司官网。
3. 一键把 lead 推进到跟进链路。
4. 它会进入你本地的 Customer、Quote 和 Task。
5. 在 Today Queue 里看今天最该先推哪一笔。
6. 用 Quick Follow-up 持续推进报价。
7. 在 Timeline 里看每次变化，必要时还能撤回。
8. 数据默认留在本机，外部 AI 接口默认关闭。
